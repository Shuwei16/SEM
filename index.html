FOCS is dedicated to provide quality education and training in the field of computing to prepare our students for successful careers in the industry. With a wide range of programs and state-of-the-art facilities, FOCS is committed to bringing out the best in every student and helping them become leaders in the rapidly evolving world of technology.

FOCS is divided into five departments:

  • Department of Computer Science And Embedded Systems (DCSE)
  • Department of Information And Communication Technology (DICT)
  • Department of Information Systems And Security (DISS)
  • Department of Mathematical And Data Science (DMDS)
  • Department of Software Engineering And Technology (DSET)
