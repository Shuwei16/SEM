Biography

I am Encik Ooi Chun Chun, and I am honored to hold the position of Deputy Dean (Academic) while also serving as a Senior Lecturer at TAR University College's Faculty of Computing and Information Technology (FOCS). My expertise and passion revolve around the fascinating realm of Information and Communication Technology Management.

In my journey through academia, I have amassed a robust educational foundation and practical experience in the field. My mission is clear: to ensure that FOCS upholds the highest standards of academic excellence. It is my privilege to guide and inspire our students, equipping them with the knowledge and skills needed to thrive in the dynamic world of ICT management.

Within the vibrant academic community of FOCS, my role as Deputy Dean allows me to shape the curriculum, foster research initiatives, and maintain an environment that promotes innovation and growth. As a Senior Lecturer, I have the pleasure of engaging with students directly, sharing insights, and encouraging them to reach their full potential in this ever-evolving field.

My dedication to academic excellence and my commitment to innovative teaching methods make me deeply passionate about my work. I am proud to contribute to the development of future ICT professionals at TARUC's FOCS. Together, we embark on a journey of learning and discovery, preparing our students for the exciting challenges that lie ahead in the world of Information and Communication Technology Management.
