Biography

I'm Puan Choy Lai Lai, proudly serving as the Associate Dean for Quality Assurance and a Senior Lecturer in the Faculty of Computing and Information Technology (FOCS) at TAR University College (TARUC). My areas of expertise encompass ICT management, e-commerce, and computer systems architecture. In my role, I'm dedicated to upholding the highest academic standards, ensuring that our programs deliver exceptional quality. As a Senior Lecturer, I have the privilege of mentoring and educating our students, guiding them towards success in these dynamic fields. Together with the FOCS community, we strive to equip our students with the knowledge and skills necessary for thriving in the ever-evolving world of information technology.
